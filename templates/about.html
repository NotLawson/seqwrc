About the SEQWRC

The South East Queensland Worst Running club is a developing run club that welcomes beginners, casual runners, and those who run for fun rather than competition, based in Ipswitch.
This website serves as a hub for organizing runs, tracking progress, and fostering a supportive and lighthearted running culture.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a supportive and inclusive environment for runners of all levels to come together, share their love of running, and improve their fitness and health.

We aim to foster a sense of community and camaraderie among our members, and to provide a platform for runners to connect, share their experiences, and motivate each other to achieve their running goals.
